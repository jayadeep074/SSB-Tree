Products from Trees

You may be surprised by how many products you use on a daily basis that actually come from trees! Many people think primarily about lumber and paper products but there’s so much more. Forest products aren’t just wood products but vary from food to building supplies to toys to instruments to packaging. These products are of great economic value and they all are drivers to continue to sustainably manage our valuable renewable forests.
